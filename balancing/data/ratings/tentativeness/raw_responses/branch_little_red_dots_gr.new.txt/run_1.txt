**1. Debater A: [Opening] (Left)**
"Black hole accretion in AGN (active galactic nuclei) is a **highly plausible** explanation for LRDs"

**Rating: 2** — One hedging expression ("highly plausible") that is noticeable but the claim is otherwise direct.
