Question

A ball of ice 50 cm in diameter floats in a bathtub that holds 200 L water, which is brim full with very cold water (0°). The ice has a 50 cm^3 railroad spike frozen inside it. When it melts, what will happen?


a) The bathtub will stay brim full

b) The water level in the bathtub will fall

c) The water in the bathtub will spill over


You may ignore the effects of differences in an object's weight in air vs. vacuum or other considerations that the question author would be unlikely to consider relevant. In other words, please provide the answer that you believe the question author would be most likely to mark as correct.
